General Questions

What is Brava?

Brava is a premium streetwear brand specializing in high-quality t-shirts with unique designs. We focus on creating bold, expressive clothing that allows individuals to showcase their personal style.

Where are your t-shirts made?

Our t-shirts are made using premium materials and are manufactured in facilities that meet our high quality standards. We prioritize ethical manufacturing practices and sustainable materials.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping rates and delivery times vary by location. You can check shipping options during checkout.

Order Questions

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can also contact our customer service team with your order number for assistance.

Can I change or cancel my order?

To change or cancel an order, please contact us as soon as possible. Once an order has been shipped, it cannot be canceled, but you can return it according to our return policy.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay. All payments are processed securely.

Shipping Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within the US. International shipping can take 7-14 business days depending on the destination.

Do you offer express shipping?

Yes, we offer express shipping options for faster delivery. Express shipping typically takes 1-2 business days within the US.

How much does shipping cost?

Shipping costs vary based on your location and the shipping method you choose. Free shipping is available on orders over $50 within the US.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on unused items in original packaging. Please visit our Terms and Conditions page for complete details on returns and exchanges.

How do I return an item?

To return an item, please contact our customer service team with your order number. We'll provide you with a return shipping label and instructions.

Can I exchange for a different size?

Yes, you can exchange for a different size within 30 days of purchase. Please contact our customer service team to arrange the exchange.

Product Questions

What materials are your t-shirts made from?

Our t-shirts are made from premium 100% cotton or cotton blends for maximum comfort and durability. We also use sustainable materials when possible.

How do I care for my t-shirt?

We recommend washing in cold water and tumble drying on low heat. Avoid using bleach or fabric softeners to preserve the design and fabric quality.

Are your designs limited edition?

Some of our designs are limited edition and may not be restocked. We clearly mark limited edition items on our product pages.

Still have questions?

Our customer service team is here to help you with any additional questions or concerns.
