Find Your Perfect Fit

Our size guide is specifically designed for Moroccan body types to help you choose the perfect size for your Brava t-shirt.

How to Measure

  • 1

    Chest Measurement

    Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape horizontal and snug but not tight.

  • 2

    Shoulder Width

    Measure from the tip of one shoulder to the tip of the other shoulder across your back.

  • 3

    Length

    Measure from the base of your neck (where the collar would sit) down to your desired length.

  • 4

    Sleeve Length

    For short sleeves, measure from the shoulder seam to where you want the sleeve to end.

Our sizes are based on Moroccan body measurements. If you're between sizes, we recommend going up one size for a more comfortable fit.

Fit Guide

Regular Fit: Standard fit with comfortable room around chest and shoulders. Perfect for everyday wear.

Choosing Your Size

For a Relaxed Fit

Choose one size larger than your chest measurement indicates. This gives you extra room for comfort and layering.

For a Standard Fit

Choose the size that matches your chest measurement. This provides a classic, comfortable fit.

For a Fitted Look

Choose one size smaller for a more fitted appearance. Perfect if you prefer a closer-to-body fit.

Consider Your Climate

In Morocco's warm climate, you might prefer a slightly looser fit for better airflow and comfort.
